The Loonaverse

Everything flipped and we started the journey through the FREE –

The Loonaverse is a universe that exists on a möbius strip. 12 girls trying to find each other, trapped in a time loop to repeat forever unless the cycle is broke. Our story begins on earth with the creation of color by the first girl, Heejin. From there the 11 girls are brought together by fate to destroy the moon and construct a new moon to escape the coming loop.

About

LOOΠΔ 이달의 소녀 is a South Korean twelve member group formed by Blockberry Creative. They were introduced to the public through a pre-debut project that began in October 2016, where each of the members was revealed in a periodic fashion by releasing a promotional single over the following 18 months. They debuted as a full ensemble with the extended play, [+ +] in 2018. Not just a music group, LOOΠΔ are main characters in the Sci-Fi Thriller, The LOONAVERSE, a multiple part chronolgy depicting the 12 girls meeting and escaping a time loop.

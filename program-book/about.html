About

The VCD Program Book is a complete collection of all projects in the Graphic Design program at Arizona State University.

Crescendo explores Alyssa Herrera’s journey from a timid visionary to a confident and focused designer.

Preface


Before design, there were other paths that I had envisioned myself seriously pursuing: math, writing, nature, and most notably, music.

Music has always been – and will always remain – a love of my life. It continues to be my muse, the catalyst of my findings, a wonderful inner voice which has influenced my design work to go further, moving from my timid beginnings to courageous resolutions in an endless crescendo.

This book is an ode to that passion and all the other paths. It is a statement that everything I’ve ever loved has always been a part of the grand design.

Crescendo

Alyssa Herrera // Spring 2023

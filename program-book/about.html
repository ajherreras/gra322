About

The VCD Program Book is a complete collection of all projects in the Graphic Design program at Arizona State University.

Crescendo explores Alyssa Herrera’s journey from a timid visionary to a confident and focused designer.

Before design, there were many smaller passions, various ‘loves’ that I had envisioned myself seriously pursuing: math, poetry, nature, drawing, music. When I had grasped fully the gravity of what I wanted to do, I realized I had always picked apart those passions – I was enthused by their functionings, solaced by their forms; I was always a designer.

Everything stayed as small passions that still find themselves intertwined in my work as a designer. Music, most notably, has always been – and will always remain – a love of my life. Music continues to be my muse, the catalyst of my findings, a wonderful inner voice which has influenced my design work to go further, moving from my timid beginnings to courageous resolutions in an endless crescendo.

This book is an ode to my passions. Every influence can be seen in my academic work. Everything I’ve ever loved has always been a part of the grand design.

Crescendo

Alyssa Herrera // Spring 2023
